Job Description

We are seeking an exceptional Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our world-class AI research team at OpenAI. In this role, you will lead the development of cutting-edge machine learning models that push the boundaries of artificial intelligence. You will work closely with researchers to implement novel algorithms and deploy them at scale across our products and services.

The ideal candidate has deep expertise in deep learning, natural language processing, and large-scale model training. You will be responsible for designing and building ML infrastructure that enables breakthrough research in AGI development.

Responsibilities

  • Lead the design and implementation of large-scale machine learning models for AI research
  • Collaborate with research scientists to translate research findings into production systems
  • Build and optimize ML infrastructure for training and deploying models at scale
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to technical documentation
  • Stay current with latest ML research and apply innovations to real-world problems

Required Skills

Python PyTorch TensorFlow Deep Learning NLP Computer Vision ML Infrastructure CUDA Distributed Training MLOps Git Docker

Qualifications

  • PhD or Master's degree in Computer Science, ML, AI, or related field
  • 5+ years of experience building production ML systems
  • Strong publication record in top ML conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, etc.)
  • Experience with large-scale distributed training systems
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
